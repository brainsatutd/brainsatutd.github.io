Overview

CAIR is an AI/ML research group at The University of Texas at Dallas that focuses on cutting-edge machine learning research and publication. Our teams work on advancing SOTA architectures and models through innovative research projects and collaborations. We welcome students and researchers from all backgrounds who are passionate about artificial intelligence. Our group meets regularly to discuss recent AI/ML papers and advance our own research initiatives. Our goal is to foster an inclusive, collaborative environment where members can gain hands-on research experience and contribute to the advancement of research in our fields.

Research Focus

Computer Vision, Neurosymbolic AI, Audio and Speech Processing, Multimodal Learning, and more

Collaborative Environment

Inclusive research group welcoming students and researchers from all disciplines

Innovation

Advancing state-of-the-art AI/ML through cutting-edge research and publications

Recruitment

CAIR is always looking for passionate researchers and students to join our team! We encourage students and researchers from all disciplines to get involved. If you're interested in joining our research group or collaborating on projects, please reach out to us through our contact information below.

We welcome researchers at all levels - from undergraduate to doctoral students.
